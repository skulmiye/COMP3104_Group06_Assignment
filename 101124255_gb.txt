George Brown College is located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada

Was established in 1967 and is named after George Brown, a prominent Canadian 
journalist and politician who was one of the Fathers of Confederation

The college offers a wide range of programs, 
including diploma, certificate, degree, and postgraduate programs

The college is involved in research and innovation initiatives, 
working on projects that benefit the community and industry.
