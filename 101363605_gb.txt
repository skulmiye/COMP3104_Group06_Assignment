- George Brown College -
George Brown (1818-1880) was a prominent Canadian politician, newspaper founder, and one of the Fathers of Confederation. 

George Brown College, named in his honor in 1967, 
opened in 1968 with just over 2,000 students. It is located in Toronto, Ontario 

Initially offering programs in various fields, the college has since grown to around 30,000 students 
and expanded its international reach, contributing to Toronto's cultural and economic development since 2004.
